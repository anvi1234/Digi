About Digital Marketing

The Advanced Excel Training by Internshala is a 4-week online training program covering the basic and advanced modules of Microsoft Excel. In this training program, you will learn how to use Microsoft Excel and the advanced concepts of Excel. During the course of this training, students will work on a project unit relating to 'Food2Go', a food technology startup.

Read more 
The project unit is divided into four assignments. Each assignment focuses on solving a different startup challenge using the concepts covered in the respective module. Food2Go faces a variety of challenges that include product-centric, customer-centric and strategic growth-oriented. Students will be presented with interesting real-life scenarios and have to use the Excel tools, functions and data visualization techniques learned to solve problems, make inferences and present data in a meaningful and effective manner using Excel.
